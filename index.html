About Me

I spend most of my spare time travelling. Travel is simply a way of inspiration, and years of undergraduate life in the States taught me that knowledge comes from real life. I used to travel with my parents when I was young, but now, I may travel alone, or travel with a group of close friends, surrounded by the lovely nature or the skyscrapers in big cities. Whenever I travel, I would love to observe everything around, and I have the strong feeling that I am the character in some role-playing game, taking adventure. The thought of being a game designer was born then.
